Early years

Daria Bashkova was born on April 19, 1986, in Russia, to parents Nadezhda and Alexander Bashkov. She spent her early years in her hometown of Izhevsk, surrounded by the rich history and culture of her homeland. Growing up, Daria was a curious and ambitious child, always eager to learn and explore.

The Move to Canada

In 2001, when Daria was 15 years old, her family made a life-changing decision. Faced with economic challenges and desire for new opportunities, they decided to leave Russia and immigrate to Canada. The family faced the challenges of adapting to a new culture, language, and climate in their new home, Toronto, Canada. It was a difficult transition, but they were determined to build a better life.

Why Canada, Toronto?

Toronto is one of the most culturally diverse cities in the world. Over the years it has attracted immigrants from every corner of the globe, resulting in a mosaic of cultures, languages, and traditions. People from Asia, Africa, Europe, the Caribbean, and the Middle East have made Toronto their home.

Facts about Toronto
  1. Toronto is home to the iconic CN Tower, which held the title of the world's tallest freestanding structure for over 30 years. It offers breathtaking views of the city and has a glass floor for daring visitors.
  2. Toronto boasts several major sports teams, including the Toronto Maple Leafs (NHL), Toronto Raptors (NBA), Toronto Blue Jays (MLB), and Toronto FC (MLS). The city is passionate about sports, and its teams have dedicated fan bases.
  3. Toronto is Canada's financial center and home to numerous banks, financial institutions, and corporate headquarters.

Early days in Canada

Upon arriving in Toronto, Daria had to go through the settlement process, which involved finding accommodation, opening a bank account, and obtaining necessary identification documents. Soon she enrolled in English language classes to improve her communication skills. Finding a job early on became priority for Daria. She quickly started seeking entry-level positions. Job hunting was very competitive, so she then decided to continue her education in George Brown College.

About George Brown College

George Brown College is a well-known institution of higher education located in Toronto, Ontario, Canada. The College has multiple campuses throughout downtown Toronto, offering students easy access to the city's cultural, business, and social opportunities. George Brown College offers a wide range of programs, including diploma and certificate programs, degree programs, and continuing education courses. The college covers a variety of fields, including business, health sciences, community services, design, arts, and technology.

Daria's Life Today

Today Daria's life is a dynamic journey filled with learning, challenges, and opportunities for growth. At the College, she studies a variety of subjects, including HTML, CSS, JavaScript, front-end development, and web design principles. A significant portion of a Daria's student life involves coding, which she enjoys a lot.
